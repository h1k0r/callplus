COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I even need CALLPLUS? (scenario 1)

In most cases, when you access your billing (CDR) you see just one entry with phone call.

Let’s say you see a call from number A to number B and the call lasted for 2 minutes. Callplus will show you that caller joined the queue (as 3rd in line) but had to listen to Music on Hold for 1:30 minutes. In the meantime Asterisk tried to dial eg. 100, 101 & 102 extensions but two of them were already busy and the last one was unavailable. Finally 101 became free and answered the call.

Who's your target client?

Basically anyone with Asterisk server. Currently, among our clients we have hospitals, clinigs, public institutions, call-centers, contact centers, taxi companies, etc…

Can I use this for free?

Sure! Even after beta tests there will still be a free version of a software that will allow you to view live Asterisk events.

Why do I even need CALLPLUS? (scenario 2)

Let’s say you have a call center with 10 agents. But in Callplus you see a really high AR (Abandon Rate) in your Queues. So you start to wonder?

When do they login/logout their SIP extensions?
At which position do people start in queues?
Who spend the most time talking?
Should I hire new agents to unload the traffic or just kindly ask my remote workers, not to take that many breaks?

People that use Callplus on-premise use it to answer all of the above.

How does it work?

Callplus uses AMI (Asterisk Manager Interface) to capture all event that occured on your server. Depending on the version you will be able to view and/or store historic data

What do I need?

All you need to have is an Asterisk server with AMI. You’ll need to set up some port forwarding so that we’ll be able to capture all the Events.
If you use FreePBX, IssabelPBX or alike, you’ll be able to connect Callplus with your SQL so that we’ll have detailed data based on real you configuration and not just captured data.

Is this really secure?

To move the data, Callplus uses TLS encryption and we’ll help you add manager users will read-only permissions.

What are the different versions of the software?

We’re working on that, but the idea is to have multiple versions of the software that will allow you to access more services and even fetch detailed data directly from your server.
